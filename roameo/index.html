Privacy Policy — Roameo

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Developer: Dino Bozic (dinz)

Overview

Roameo is an exploration and adventure tracking app that encourages you to discover new places. This policy explains what data we collect and how we protect your privacy. Roameo is designed to keep your exploration data private and local.

Data Collection

Data We Collect

Location Data: Roameo uses your location to track areas you've explored and to generate your exploration map. This location data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your location to our servers or share it with third parties.

Photos: When you take photos at locations, those images are stored locally on your device. Photos are not uploaded to any server unless you explicitly choose to share them through iOS sharing features.

Exploration History: Data about places you've visited, exploration milestones, and achievements are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data remains private and under your control.

Camera Access: We use your device camera only when you choose to take photos at exploration spots. Camera access is not used in the background.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not transmit your location data to any server
  • We do not upload your exploration photos to the cloud
  • We do not track your movements continuously in the background
  • We do not collect your email, name, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share your data with third parties

Location Tracking

How We Use Location:

  • To mark areas you've explored on your personal map
  • To suggest nearby unexplored areas
  • To calculate exploration statistics and achievements
  • To enable location-based features you choose to use

Background Location: If you grant "Always" location permission, Roameo can track your explorations even when the app is closed. This is optional and enhances the exploration tracking experience. You can choose "While Using" permission if you prefer to track only when the app is open.

Location Privacy: Your location data never leaves your device. We do not build location profiles or sell location data.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services: Roameo uses the following Apple services:

  • MapKit: For displaying maps. Apple may receive anonymized location requests for map tile loading according to Apple's privacy policy.
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases and premium features. Apple processes payment information according to their privacy policy.
  • iCloud (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync in iOS Settings, your exploration data may sync across your Apple devices via your private iCloud account.

No Other Services: Roameo does not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage providers (except optional iCloud)

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: All exploration data, photos, and location history are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is encrypted using iOS's built-in device encryption when you have a passcode enabled.

iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud for Roameo in iOS Settings, your exploration data syncs securely via your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

No Cloud Servers: We do not operate any cloud servers or databases that store your exploration data.

Permissions

Location: Required for core exploration tracking features. You can choose "While Using" or "Always" permission levels. You can revoke or modify this permission in iOS Settings at any time.

Camera: Optional, used only when you choose to photograph locations. Only accessed when you tap the camera button.

Photo Library: Optional, used only if you want to save exploration photos to your Photos library.

Your Rights

  • All your exploration data is on your device. You can delete individual entries, photos, or your entire exploration history at any time from within the app.
  • You can disable iCloud sync to keep data strictly local.
  • You can change location permission levels in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove all data permanently.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete it or delete the app.
  • If you use iCloud sync, data remains in your iCloud account according to Apple's retention policies until you delete it or disable sync.

Children's Privacy

Roameo is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents should be aware that the app tracks location data (stored locally) and uses the camera.

Sharing Features

Manual Sharing Only: You can choose to share exploration photos or achievements using iOS's built-in sharing features (Messages, Mail, social media, etc.). When you share, you control what is shared and with whom. We do not automatically share your exploration data with anyone.

International Users

Roameo works the same everywhere in the world. Since all data stays on your device, there are no international data transfer issues.

Advertising and Tracking

We do not display ads. We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell your data.

Battery Usage

Location tracking, especially with "Always" permission, can impact battery life. You can minimize this by using "While Using" permission or by disabling location when not actively exploring.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app if possible.

Roameo keeps your exploration journey private. Your location data and photos stay on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them. We do not transmit, store, or sell your exploration data.
