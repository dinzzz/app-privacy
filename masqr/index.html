Privacy Policy — Masqr

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Developer: Dino Bozic (dinz)

Overview

Masqr is designed with privacy as the absolute top priority. This app allows you to redact and blur sensitive information in screenshots, and it does so entirely on your device. This policy explains our commitment to your privacy.

Data Collection

Data We Collect

None. Masqr collects NO personal data whatsoever.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your photos or screenshots
  • We do not transmit your images to any server
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect your email, name, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not collect device identifiers or usage data
  • We do not create user profiles
  • We do not store any data about you or your usage

How Masqr Works

100% On-Device Processing: All image processing, redaction, and blurring happens locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's built-in image processing frameworks. Your screenshots never leave your device.

No Network Access: Masqr does not make any network calls except for StoreKit (Apple's in-app purchase framework). When you export a redacted image, it is saved directly to your Photos library without any cloud transmission.

No Accounts Required: You do not need to create an account or log in. There is no authentication system.

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit: This is the only external service Masqr interacts with, and only for processing in-app purchases or unlocking premium features. Apple processes payment information according to their privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

No Other Services: Masqr does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • AI or machine learning APIs

Data Storage & Security

Local Only: Any app preferences (like your preferred blur intensity) are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

No Cloud Backup: Since Masqr stores no sensitive data, there is nothing to back up to iCloud or any other cloud service.

Security: Your images are processed in your device's secure memory space and are immediately discarded after you save or discard the edited version.

Your Rights

Since we collect no data about you, there is no data to request, delete, or modify. You have complete control over your images at all times.

Children's Privacy

Masqr is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13, because we collect no personal information whatsoever. Parents can confidently allow their children to use Masqr without privacy concerns.

International Users

Masqr works identically everywhere in the world. Since all processing happens on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Advertising and Tracking

We do not display ads. We do not track you. We do not sell data (because we don't collect any).

Permissions

Photo Library Access: Masqr requests access to your photo library only when you choose to import a screenshot to edit or save an edited image. iOS manages this permission, and you can revoke it at any time in Settings. We only access the specific photos you choose to import.

No Other Permissions: Masqr does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, or any other device features.

Changes to This Policy

Since Masqr's privacy model is fundamentally built on collecting no data, this policy is unlikely to change. If it does, we will post updates here with an updated date.

Open Source

We are considering open-sourcing Masqr to provide full transparency about how it works. If you're interested in reviewing the code, contact us for updates.

Masqr is our most privacy-friendly app because it collects absolutely nothing. Your screenshots are yours alone, processed entirely on your device, and never transmitted anywhere.
