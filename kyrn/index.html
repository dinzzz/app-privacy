Privacy Policy — Kyrn

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Developer: Dino Bozic (dinz)

Overview

Kyrn is a gamified fitness app that transforms your real-world workouts into character progression and adventures. This policy explains our commitment to protecting your health and fitness data privacy. Your workout data and health information remain private and secure on your device.

Data Collection

Data We Collect

Fitness and Workout Data: All workout sessions, exercise logs, fitness metrics, and activity data you enter are stored locally on your device using SwiftData, Apple's secure data persistence framework. This data never leaves your device.

Character Progression Data: Your in-game character stats, levels, achievements, equipment, and game progress are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server.

HealthKit Data (Optional): If you grant permission, Kyrn can read fitness and health data from Apple Health (HealthKit) to automatically track workouts and update your character. This data is accessed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

No Account Required: Kyrn does not require account creation. There is no email, username, or password to manage.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not transmit your workout or health data to any server
  • We do not upload fitness records or health information to the cloud
  • We do not track your location during workouts
  • We do not collect your email, name, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share your fitness data with third parties
  • We do not create user profiles for advertising

Critical Privacy Promise

Your workout and health data never leaves your device. Kyrn operates entirely offline (except for optional StoreKit purchases). Your fitness information remains private, secure, and under your complete control.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services: Kyrn uses the following Apple services:

  • HealthKit: Optional integration to read fitness and health data from Apple Health. All data remains on your device. You control which data types Kyrn can access through iOS Settings.
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases and premium features. Apple processes payment information according to their privacy policy. We never see your payment details.
  • iCloud (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync for Kyrn in iOS Settings, your character and workout data may sync across your Apple devices via your private iCloud account.

No Other Services: Kyrn does not use:

  • Cloud storage providers (except optional iCloud)
  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Leaderboards or multiplayer servers

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: All fitness data and character progression are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is encrypted using iOS's built-in device encryption when you have a passcode enabled.

HealthKit Security: HealthKit data is managed by iOS and is encrypted. Kyrn accesses only the data types you explicitly authorize (typically workouts, active energy, steps, exercise minutes).

iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud for Kyrn in iOS Settings, your workout and character data syncs securely via your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

No Cloud Servers: We do not operate any cloud servers or databases that store your fitness information.

Permissions

HealthKit (Optional): Required only if you want to automatically sync workouts from Apple Health. You can deny this permission and manually log workouts instead. You control which specific data types Kyrn can read and write through iOS Settings.

Notifications (Optional): Used only to send you motivational reminders or achievement notifications. Managed by iOS and can be disabled in Settings.

No Other Permissions: Kyrn does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, microphone, or other device features.

Your Rights

  • All your fitness and character data is on your device. You can delete individual workouts, reset your character, or clear all data at any time from within the app.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access in iOS Settings at any time.
  • You can disable iCloud sync to keep data strictly local.
  • You can delete the app to remove all data permanently from your device.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete it or delete the app.
  • If you use iCloud sync, data remains in your iCloud account according to Apple's retention policies until you delete it or disable sync.
  • HealthKit data remains in Apple Health and is not affected by deleting Kyrn.

Gamification and Motivation

How It Works: Kyrn uses your workout data to level up your character, unlock equipment, and progress through adventures. All game mechanics are powered by your real fitness activities. The more you work out, the stronger your character becomes.

Privacy-Friendly Design: Unlike many fitness apps with social features, Kyrn keeps your journey private. There are no leaderboards, no social sharing requirements, and no pressure to expose your fitness data to others.

Children's Privacy

Kyrn is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data stays on the device, there are minimal privacy concerns. Parents should supervise children's use of fitness tracking apps and ensure appropriate workout intensity.

Sharing Features

Manual Sharing Only: You can choose to share achievements or workout summaries using iOS's built-in sharing features (Messages, social media, etc.). When you share, you control what is shared and with whom. We do not automatically share your fitness data.

Screenshot Sharing: You can take screenshots of your character or achievements to share. These screenshots are generated locally and contain only the information visible on screen.

Health and Safety

Not a Medical Device: Kyrn is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. It is a gamified fitness tracking tool designed for motivation and entertainment.

Exercise Safely: Always consult with a healthcare professional before starting a new fitness program. Listen to your body and exercise within your capabilities.

International Users

Kyrn works the same everywhere in the world. Since all data stays on your device, there are no international data transfer issues.

Advertising and Tracking

We do not display ads. We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell your fitness data under any circumstances.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Given Kyrn's privacy-first design (no data collection), significant changes to our core privacy model are unlikely.

Open Source Consideration

We are considering open-sourcing Kyrn to provide full transparency about how your fitness data is handled. Contact us if you're interested in reviewing the code.

Your workout and health data never leaves your device. Kyrn is built on the principle of complete fitness data privacy. All tracking, gamification, and character progression happen locally. We collect nothing. We share nothing. Your fitness journey is yours alone.
