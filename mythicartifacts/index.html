Privacy Policy — Vault of Aeon

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Developer: Dino Bozic (Dinz Studio)

Overview

Vault of Aeon is a collector game where you summon and collect mythological artifacts across 8 themed sets. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it is handled. We designed Vault of Aeon to collect the minimum data necessary to let you sign in, sync your collection across devices, process in-app purchases securely, and operate the app reliably (crash reporting, analytics, push notifications).

Data We Collect

Account Data (only if you choose to sign in)

Vault of Aeon can be played without creating an account. If you choose to sign in to sync your progress across devices, we collect the following via Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google:

  • Email address — used as your account identifier and for occasional service-critical communications (e.g., notifying you of data deletion requests).
  • Name — optional; used only for in-app display.
  • User ID (opaque identifier) — a unique ID issued by Apple or Google so we can recognize you on subsequent sign-ins.

With Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your real email address using Apple's private email relay. We respect that choice and never attempt to link the relayed address to anything else.

Collection & Progress Data

When signed in, your collection (which artifacts you own, duplicates, evolutions, set completion, meta-story chapter unlocks, daily streak, and earned seals/rewards) is synchronized to our backend so you can access it from another device or recover it after reinstalling. This data is tied to your account and contains no personal information beyond the User ID.

Purchase Data

When you make an in-app purchase, Apple provides us with a signed purchase receipt. We validate the receipt on our server to grant you the corresponding gems, subscription benefits (Mythic Pass), or lifetime features (Eternal Vault). We store the transaction identifier and product identifier to prevent duplicate crediting. We never receive your credit card, billing address, or any other payment details — those remain entirely with Apple.

Analytics & Crash Diagnostics

To improve the app and understand how features are used, Vault of Aeon uses Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics — collects aggregated, pseudonymous usage events such as which screens you view, when you summon, when you complete a set, and when you make a purchase. No precise location, contacts, or browsing data is collected. Events are tied to a Firebase-generated installation ID and, when you are signed in, to your account's user ID.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — when the app crashes or encounters a non-fatal error, an automated crash report is sent containing the device model, OS version, anonymized device identifier, app version, and a stack trace. We attach the user ID (if signed in) so we can investigate issues you report.

We have configured Firebase to not collect IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) and we do not use Firebase for advertising, ad personalization, or cross-app tracking. Data is processed by Google in accordance with the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, we register a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token with our backend so we can send daily reset reminders, Fate Compass rotations, Vault Target alerts, and similar engagement messages. The FCM token is associated with an installation ID (a UUID generated locally on first launch) and, when you are signed in, with your account. You can revoke notification permission at any time in iOS Settings; we will stop sending pushes immediately.

Device Metadata

Alongside the FCM token, we store the platform (iOS), app version, build number, and locale so we can target pushes correctly (e.g., not send a "new feature" notification to users on an older version) and so analytics segmentation is meaningful. None of this is personally identifying.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, or date of birth
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect contacts, calendar, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • We do not use Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or similar advertising identifiers
  • We do not use advertising networks or serve ads
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

How Data Is Used

  • App functionality: to authenticate your account, sync your collection, validate in-app purchases, and deliver notifications you have opted in to.
  • Service integrity: to prevent fraud, duplicate purchases, and abuse of the in-app economy.
  • Diagnostics: to detect crashes and performance issues so we can fix them.
  • Product improvement: to understand which features are used and how to make the game better.
  • Support: if you email us for help, we may look up your account by email to investigate the issue.

We do not use your data for advertising, ad personalization, profiling for marketing purposes, or any purpose unrelated to running Vault of Aeon.

Third-Party Services

Vault of Aeon relies on the following third parties only to provide the app:

  • Apple (Sign in with Apple, StoreKit, push notifications, App Store receipt validation) — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Google (Sign in with Google) — only if you choose this sign-in method. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging) — analytics events, crash reports, and push token routing. Governed by the Firebase Terms and the Google Privacy Policy. Data is processed in Google's infrastructure, primarily in the United States.
  • Railway (hosting provider) — our backend at api.dinz.io runs on Railway's infrastructure. Railway processes request traffic as a data processor; data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

We do not use Mixpanel, Amplitude, Facebook SDK, AdMob, Adjust, AppsFlyer, or any advertising or attribution SDK.

Data Storage, Transfer & Security

On-device: App settings, audio preferences, a cached copy of your collection, and an installation UUID are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and the file system.

Backend: Account data, collection data, purchase transaction IDs, and FCM device tokens are stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. All traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).

Firebase data: Analytics events and crash reports are stored in Google's Firebase infrastructure, primarily in the United States. Push tokens routed through FCM are likewise handled by Google.

Location of data: Our backend is currently hosted in the United States. If you use the app from another region, your data is transferred to and processed in the U.S. for the sole purpose of running the service.

Retention: We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (see below), we delete all associated data within 30 days. Firebase Analytics events are retained for up to 14 months by default; we do not extend this. Crashlytics retains crash reports for up to 90 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data associated with your account — email us to request a copy.
  • Correct inaccurate information — email us with corrections.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — use Settings → Delete Account inside the app, or email us. This also unregisters your FCM token from our backend.
  • Opt out of analytics: if you want to disable Firebase Analytics for your install, email us — we will mark your installation ID for exclusion. (We don't currently expose an in-app toggle.)
  • Stop using the service at any time by uninstalling the app.

Users in the EU/UK have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to object to processing. Users in California have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (we do not sell your data). We honor these rights worldwide — email us to exercise them.

Children's Privacy

Vault of Aeon is rated 12+ in the App Store due to simulated gambling mechanics (gacha / loot crate style summoning). The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we will delete the account.

In-App Purchases & Simulated Gambling

Vault of Aeon includes optional in-app purchases for gems and subscription benefits. Artifacts are acquired through randomized summoning ("pulls") that feature six rarity tiers and a transparent pity system. All odds are disclosed in-app on the Summon screen. No purchase is required to play — every artifact is obtainable through free daily pulls and regular gameplay. Virtual items have no cash value and cannot be exchanged outside the app.

Permissions

Vault of Aeon requests the following permission only:

  • Notifications — optional. If granted, we send daily reset reminders, Fate Compass rotation alerts, and similar in-game messages. You can revoke this any time in iOS Settings.

Vault of Aeon does not request access to your camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, calendar, location, or any other sensitive device feature.

Summary

Vault of Aeon collects only what is needed to run the game: your email, name, and user ID (via Sign in with Apple or Google) so you can sign in and sync your collection; your collection and progress so it can move with you across devices; Apple's purchase receipts so we can deliver what you buy; pseudonymous analytics events and crash reports via Firebase so we can improve the game and fix bugs; and a push notification token so we can send the in-game reminders you've opted in to.
