Privacy Policy — ParallelLives

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Developer: Dino Bozic (dinz)

Overview

ParallelLives is an entertainment app that lets you explore alternate versions of your life through AI-generated scenarios. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data Collection

Data We Collect

Profile Information (Local Only): When you set up your profile, you provide a name, age, and career. This data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to any server.

AI-Generated Content: When using the AI life generation feature, your profile information may be sent to our API proxy to generate personalized content. We do not store these requests or the generated responses on our servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email, phone number, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not create user accounts or require login
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

How ParallelLives Works

Local-First Design: Your collection of alternate lives, merge history, favorites, credits, and all app preferences are stored locally on your device. No cloud sync or backup is performed.

AI Generation (Optional): Life scenarios can be generated using AI via our secure API proxy. In the current version, the app operates entirely with pre-built content and does not make network calls for content generation.

No Accounts Required: You do not need to create an account or log in. There is no authentication system.

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit: Used for processing in-app purchases (credits and subscriptions). Apple processes payment information according to their privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

No Other Services: ParallelLives does not use:

  • Analytics services (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media SDKs

Data Storage & Security

Local Only: All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This includes your profile, collected lives, merge results, credits, and preferences.

Data Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time through the "Delete Account" option in Settings. This permanently removes all locally stored data. Alternatively, deleting the app removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

ParallelLives does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated for general audiences and contains no inappropriate content. Since we collect minimal data and store it only on-device, the app is safe for users of all ages.

International Users

ParallelLives works identically everywhere in the world. Since data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored only on your device, you have complete control over it at all times. You can delete all data through Settings or by removing the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will post updates here with an updated date. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notice.
