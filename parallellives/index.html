Privacy Policy

ParallelLives

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

ParallelLives lets you explore alternate versions of your life across infinite parallel universes. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how little data we collect -- which is essentially none.

ParallelLives collects no personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Collection

Data We Collect

None. ParallelLives does not collect any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact details
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers or usage analytics
  • We do not create user profiles on any server
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • We do not send your profile or life data to external servers

How ParallelLives Works

Local Storage: Your profile information, discovered lives, collection data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

AI Generation: When generating alternate lives, the app sends minimal context to OpenAI's API to create creative content. No personally identifiable information is transmitted -- only the parameters needed to generate a life scenario.

No Accounts Required: You do not need to create an account or sign in. There is no authentication system or user database.

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit: Used exclusively for processing in-app purchases (portal credits, Infinite Pass subscription). Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

OpenAI API: Used to generate alternate life scenarios. Requests contain only creative prompt parameters, not personal data. OpenAI's data usage policy applies to API interactions.

No Other Services: ParallelLives does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising networks, or cloud storage services.

Data Storage & Security

Local Only: All app data (your profile, discovered lives, collection, credits) is stored locally on your device via UserDefaults. This data is sandboxed within the app.

Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time through Settings > Delete Account. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

ParallelLives does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

International Users

ParallelLives works identically worldwide. Since data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Your Rights

Since we collect no data about you, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. You maintain complete control over your data at all times through the app's settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since our model is fundamentally built on not collecting data, significant changes are unlikely.
